# Intel RealSense D455 Depth Camera - Technical Specifications

## Manufacturer Information
- **Manufacturer**: Intel Corporation
- **Product Line**: Intel® RealSense™ Depth Camera D400 Series
- **Model**: D455

## Technical Specifications

### Depth Camera Specifications

- **Depth Field of View (FOV)**: 86° ±3° × 57° ±3° (H × V) [1][2]
- **Depth Resolution**: Up to 1280 × 720 [1][2]
- **Depth Frame Rate**: Up to 90 fps [1][2]
- **Depth Technology**: Stereo cameras with Global Shutter [1][2]
- **Recommended Range**: 0.4m to 6m [1][2]
- **Depth Accuracy**: <2% Z error at 4m [3]
- **Baseline**: 95mm [3]

### RGB Camera Specifications

- **RGB Field of View (FOV)**: 86° ±3° × 57° ±3° (H × V) [1][2]
- **RGB Resolution**: Up to 1280 × 800 [1][2]
- **RGB Frame Rate**: Up to 90 fps [1][2]
- **RGB Sensor Type**: Global Shutter [1][2]

### Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU)

- **IMU Model**: Bosch BMI055 [1][2]

### Physical Specifications

- **Dimensions**: 124mm × 26mm × 29mm [1][2]
- **Mounting Points**: Two M3 × 0.5 mounting points, and one 1/4-20 UNC thread mounting point [1]

### Connectivity

- **Connection Type**: USB Type-C 3.1 Gen 1 [1][2]

### Environmental Specifications

- **Use Environment**: Indoor/outdoor use [1][2]
